Introducing the AWC!

The AWC is a fully autonomous, non-destructive solution for eliminating weeds in a home garden. Using computer vision and machine learning technologies, the AWC is able to scan its surroundings, identify nearby weeds, and navigate towards them. A unique weed removal mechanism was designed to pull out weeds entirely, including their root system, without the use of harmful chemicals. This ensures that weeds that are pulled out will not grow back, with minimal impact to the surrounding garden.
